3. Robotic Process Automation: new computer technology

Automates Jobs- Just like AI and Machine learning, Robotic Process Automation is defined as another technology that automates several jobs. It is the use of software to automate business processes like interpreting applications, processing transactions, dealing with data, and even reporting to emails. The technology of RPA includes automation of repetitive tasks that are often used by people.

Generates More Activities- Moreover, these are not just the menial tasks of the low-paid worker and it can generate more than 45% of the activities that can be easily automated and it includes the work of financial managers, doctors as well as CFOs.

Impacted Many Lives- It has been found by Forrester Research estimates that RPA automation will threaten the livelihood of more than 230 million or knowledge workers by impacting the workforce of around 9% of people around the globe.

High Demand for Automation-It has been found out by McKinsey that less than 5% of the occupations can be automated whereas more than 60% of the occupations can be automated partially.

Plenty of Opportunities- If you are an IT professional who is looking for a brighter future and tries to understand the technological trends then RPA offers plenty of career opportunities that include developers, project managers, business analysts, solution architects, and consultants too.

It has been found by SimplyHired.com that the average RPA salary is estimated to be near around USD 1,41,000 annually. If you are eager to learn more about RPA then enrolling in the certifications of the Robotic Process Automation course should be your next step.
